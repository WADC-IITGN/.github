## About Metis

Metis is a community of passionate individuals interested in web and app development. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a beginner, everyone is welcome. Our goal is to create a collaborative environment where members can learn, share ideas, and work on exciting projects together.

## Club Goals

1. **Learning and Skill Development:** Provide resources and opportunities for members to enhance their web and app development skills.

2. **Project Collaboration:** Foster a collaborative environment where members can work on real-world projects to apply their skills and build a strong portfolio.

3. **Community Building:** Create a supportive and inclusive community that encourages knowledge sharing, mentorship, and a passion for technology.

## How to Get Involved

1. **Participate in Projects:** Contribute to ongoing projects or propose new ones. We keep on releasing new projects. **PS. Winter Projects are Coming Soon**

2. **Regular Workshops:** Attend workshops and training sessions organized by the club to enhance your technical skills and stay current with industry best practices.
